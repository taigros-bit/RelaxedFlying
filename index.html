The science
✈ Aircraft physics
Where you sit changes everything.
An aircraft behaves like a seesaw. The further from the center of gravity, the more you feel turbulence. Wing seats are right at that center — calmest. Rear seats amplify every bump 2–3×.

🌤 Turbulence increasing
Climate change is making flights bumpier.
Clear-air turbulence over the North Atlantic has increased 55%+ since the 1970s. Knowing where to sit matters more every year.
💡 Pro tip
Morning flights are smoother.
Air is denser in early morning. Afternoon solar heating creates thermal turbulence. Go early whenever possible.
